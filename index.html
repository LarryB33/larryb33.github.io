    St. Patrick's Day FAQ

    Click here for everything you need to know!
    1. Q. Where can I park? A. Free parking is available next door at Seabreeze Amusement Park. Please be respectful.
    2. Q. Is there handicap parking available? A. There will be handicap parking available on Seaview Ave. Just ask a parking attendant where to go.
    3. Q. Is there a cover charge? A. Yes, there is a cover charge for this event starting at 10:00am. This applies even if you are just coming to eat.
    5. Q. Is there an age restriction? A. The Cruel Summer concert is all ages, but a chaperone is required for each person under 21. The Queen Flash concert is 21+. Our weekend events will be all ages during the day (especially our Kid's Day event on March 16th) but will be 21+ after 7:00 pm.
    6. Q. What time are you open? A. We open at 8:00am. The live music starts at 10:00am on Saturday and 8:30am on Sunday (see above for lineup).
    7. Q. What time will you be serving food? A. We will be serving breakfast from 8:00-10:00 on Sunday while supplies last. Our Irish menu will be available from 11:00-9:00 all weekend. (see photos for menus)
    8. Q. Can I get food to go? A. Unfortunately, no. We will not be able to accomodate to-go orders on the 16th or 17th.
    9. Q. Can I make a reservation? A. We will not be able to take reservations. We apologize for any inconvenience.

                                            Our Story

                                            Shamrock Jack’s Irish Pub is an Irish family-owned Irish Pub and Restaurant blending the magical spirit of Ireland with contemporary food and drink. We are located just a few minutes north of the City of Rochester in Sea Breeze, New York, a bustling resort community on the shores of beautiful Lake Ontario.

                                            Spend the afternoon sipping pints on our expanded front porch or our back patio. Enjoy live music until the wee hours in our charming Irish pub, or have a delightful dinner with family and friends in one of our warm, welcoming dining rooms. Don’t forget to visit our resident Leprechaun just inside the front entrance!

                                            We have won 2 awards - one for best Irish Pub in Rochester and the other for best late night menu - we never forget that our customers make us what we are and for that we are forever gratefull.

                                            Come on in and try our famous delicious boneless wings and enjoy a pint with one of the Family.
